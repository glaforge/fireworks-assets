About this site

This fireworks listing is powered by APISpark, the API platform provided by Restlet. We hand-picked fireworks taking place in all the US sates, and we let you submit new ones in you area. The data collected is hosted in an APISpark Google Sheets wrapper. The fireworks that you submit are going through a Google Form that appends data to our spreadsheet. APISpark is then responsible for exposing the data from this Google Sheets as a Web API. All the assets of this site are also hosted through an APISpark file store, served by our platform. The design is coming from the Materialize project, while the images are free for reuse, coming from the Wikimedia foundation and from Flickr.
